Our Story

Cricket CRM was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to help cricket academies manage their operations efficiently. We saw that many academies struggled with manual processes for tracking players, managing fees, and coordinating coaches.

Our team of cricket enthusiasts and software developers came together to create a comprehensive solution that addresses all the challenges faced by cricket academies. Today, Cricket CRM is trusted by over 500 academies across India.

Our Mission

To empower cricket academies with technology that simplifies management, enhances player development, and drives growth. We believe that by streamlining administrative tasks, coaches and administrators can focus more on what matters most - nurturing young talent.

Our Vision

To become the leading cricket academy management platform globally, helping academies of all sizes unlock their full potential through innovative technology solutions.
